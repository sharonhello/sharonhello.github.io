(full project coming soon) Club Quarantine is a weekly virtual queer club party hosted over zoom. CQ is a space for people to get together and positively distract themselves. Their instagram is full of memes and funny posts that speak to what Club Quarantine is about. I created a very loose visual identity for CQ, using code.
