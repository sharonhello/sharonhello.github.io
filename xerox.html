Experiments in science refers to a test with an unpredictable outcome, to further advance research and put more context or knowledge onto the table; where as when an experiment in art refers to the idea that it supposed to be bad or it is something you are only testing.

My exploration with the photocopier started out with initial experimentation of how to use the Xerox machine, things I tested out were:

1. What happens when I have the cover all the way up / half up / completely down

2. What happens when I move an object along with a scanner / against it

3. Is there a certain movement I make to produce a certain curve

4. Can certain curves possibly be letter forms
